Relaxation and Grounding Exercises

Box Breathing

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds

Repeat for 2–3 minutes.

5–4–3–2–1 Grounding Technique

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can feel
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Slowly tense each muscle group for 5 seconds, then relax. Move from feet to head.

Thought Dump Exercise

Write down everything worrying you without organizing. Close the list and revisit later.
